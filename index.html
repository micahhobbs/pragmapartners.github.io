About us

We are a boutique consultancy that specialises in service design and implementation.

We design services that are clear, implementable and user focused. As a trusted partner, we do this by intimately understanding our clients needs.

Pragma is the shortening of, and Greek word for pragmatic. Being pragmatic means dealing with things sensibly and realistically. Pragmatism means responding to the world as it is, not theoretically.

We deliver tangible and actionable steps to enable success. Our clients love working with us – and we’d love to partner with you.
